Chicken Kabobs

A Weight Watchers Recipe
„Äú

  • üç≤ Feeds: 4
  • üïë Active time: 20 min
  • üïë Total time: 30 min

Ingredients:

  • 3 cups fresh basil
  • 3 tablespoons water
  • About 2 tablespoons lemon juice, more or less to taste
  • 2 teaspoons olive oil
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Red pepper flakes
  • 1.25 pounds raw boneless skinless chicken, cut into sixteenths
  • 1 each medium red and green bell peppers, cut into 2cm squares
  • 1 medium zucchini, raw, cut in half lengthwise and then in eights
  • Red onion, cut into 16 pieces
  • 2 cups cream cheese spread
  • 2 tablespoons shredded parmesan cheese

Recipe:

  • In a NutriBullet or food processer, blend
    basil, water, lemon juice, oil, garlic, salt, black pepper,
    and
    red pepper flakes
  • Combine ingredients until smooth
  • Put two tablespoons of sauce into a large bowl
  • Combine
    chicken, bell pepper, zucchini
    and
    red onion
    with the sauce, tossing until everything is coated
  • Add the
    cream cheese/parmesan cheese
    to the blender with the remaining sauce
  • Blend until smooth, and then scrape into a serving bowl
  • Heat up grill
  • While grill is heating up, soak skewers in water (if wooden) for at least 20 minutes
  • Thread chicken and vegetables evenly on 8 skewers
  • Grill skewers 8-10 minutes until chicken is no longer pink in the middle
  • Put two skewers on each plate, sprinkling with
    salt and pepper
    to taste
  • Serve plates with creamy pesto on the side

Whitefish Plate

A Rachael Ray Recipe

  • üïë Active time: 35 min
  • üïë Total time: 45 min

Ingredients:

  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 large chopped onion
  • Salt and pepper
  • 4 slices turkey bacon
  • 4 7-oz cod fillets
  • 1 pound asparagus, cut into small diagonals
  • 1 tablespoon dijon mustard
  • 1 tablespoon finely chopped fresh thyme
  • 1 almost-ripe red tomato

Recipe:

  • Heat 3 tbsp
    olive oil
    in a skillet over medium-high heat
  • Add
    onion, salt
    and
    pepper
    stirring occasionaly for 15-20 minutes
  • Meanwhile, pat
    cod
    dry and season with
    salt & pepper
  • After onions turn golden-brown, remove from pan and keep warm
  • Add more oil to the skillet, putting in the
    cod and turkey bacon
  • Cook each fish fillet for 4 minutes each side, and the turkey bacon for the same
  • Meanwhile, boil 1 inch of water in another skillet, adding
    salt & asparagus
    for 3 minutes
  • Drain asparagus, then add
    dijon mustard, thyme,
    and
    onion mixture,
    tossing to mix
  • Put a layer of asparagus evenly among 4 plates, then the bacon followed by the cod
  • Slice
    tomato
    to get four slices, putting one on top of each cod
  • Serve immediately

Breakfast Bread

An Original Recipie
„Äú

  • üç≤ Feeds: 2
  • üïë Active time: 10 min
  • üïë Total time: 15 min

Ingredients:

  • 2 large eggs
  • Some white vinegar
  • 2 strips turkey bacon
  • 1/2 tube of soyrizo
  • 2 slices whole-wheat bread
  • Small avocado
  • Salt and pepper

Recipe:

  • Bring a pot of water to a simmer, about 2 quarts
  • Add a splash of
    vinegar
  • Crack one
    egg
    into a small bowl, then quickly drop it into the water
  • Repeat with the other egg, spooning out after 3 minutes (should be liquid inside)
  • Put the eggs on a plate with a paper towel, cutting off the wispy edges
  • Meanwhile, put the
    soyrizo
    into a pan on medium heat, stirring constantly until cooked (should be brown)
  • Put two slices of
    turkey bacon
    into the pan, letting them sit until they reach desired crispines
  • Meanwhile, toast
    bread
    on medium for about 4 minutes
  • Slice the
    avocado,
    setting it to the side
  • Once the turkey bacon is done, slice each piece in half
  • Sprinkle each slice of toast with half the soyrizo, then lay two halves of turkey bacon on top
  • Place the avocado on next, followed by the poached egg
  • Sprinkle with
    salt and pepper
    then serve
