Let's Get Cooking!

Welcome to my little cooking website. This site was created to show you, a budding chef, how to create delicious dishes using many different proteins and styles. The different subpages are linked above. A little overview of the different dishes is as follows: For the steak, I have recipes for steak diane, seared steak over white wine mushrooms and caramelized onions, and grilled steak with charred bell peppers and onions. In the turkey bacon section, there is a whitefish and turkey bacon over a bed of dijon asparagus along with a poached egg, chorizo, avocado, and turkey bacon over toast. The chicken plate is a grilled chicken kabob with onions, cucumber, and pepper with a spinach pesto. The dessert section has fresh snow ice cream and a guava smoothie, while the pastries are giant molasses cookies sprinkled with sugar, along with a loaf of banana bread.
