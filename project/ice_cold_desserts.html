    salt
  • Split the dough into twelve sections. Round each section into a sphere, then push down until the cookies are just thick enough that you can lift them
  • Put the cookies on a cookie tray over parchment paper, as far apart as possible
  • Cover each cookie with 1/12 of the

  • Allow cookies to cool for 20 minutes, and then cut apart if they stuck together

Banana Bread

An Allrecipes Recipe
„Äú

  • üç≤ Feeds: 12
  • üïë Active time: 20 min
  • üïë Total time: 100 min

Ingredients:

  • 3/4 cups brown sugar, packed
  • 2 and 1/3 cups overripe bananas (mashed)
  • 2 large eggs, beaten
  • 2 cups of flour (all-purpose)
  • 1 teaspoons of baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cups of butter

Recipe:

  • Preheat oven to 350 degrees fahrenheit
  • Spray a 9x5 inch loaf pan with Pam for Baking
  • Put
    flour, baking soda
    and
    salt
    in a bowl, mixing until combined
  • Add
    butter
    and
    brown sugar
    . Then, add
    eggs
    and
    banana
    , stirring until mashed.
  • Fold the banana mix into the flour mix, stirring until the mix is moist
  • Pour batter into the loaf pan
  • Cover each cookie with 1/12 of the
    sugar
    (more if you want)
  • Bake 60-65 minutes, until a toothpick comes out clean
  • Pull the bread out of the oven, but DO NOT try to take it out of the pan
  • After 10 minutes of cooling, flip the tray over onto a plate to serve. Flip over again to stand it up

Snow Cream

An Original Recipie
„Äú

  • üç≤ Feeds: 4
  • üïë Active time: 15 min
  • üïë Total time: 15 min

Ingredients:

  • 8ish cups of fresh snow
  • 2 cups fine white sugar
  • 1 1/2 tablespoon of vanilla extract
  • A few cups of milk
  • Pinch of salt
  • Colorful sprinkes

Recipe:

  • Mix together
    sugar, extract, milk,
    and
    salt
    until they are one liquid
  • Go outside, collecting a big bowl of fresh snow in a new bowl
  • Right away, start adding the
    snow
    to the milky liquid. Work quickly, or the snow will melt
  • Keep adding snow until the ice cream has an ice-cream like consistency. It should be fluffy not runny
  • The snow cream will not look just like ice cream, but that doesn't mean it isn't correct
  • As soon as the texture is right, scoop into bowls
  • Put on some
    sprinkles,
    then serve immediately
