Steak Diane

A Gordon Ramasay Recipie

Ingredients:

  • 4 small sirloin steaks, about 7 oz.
  • 3 peeled shallots or pearl onions
  • 4 ounces mushrooms of choice- sliced and cut into thin strips
  • 1 tablespoon salted butter
  • 1 garlic clove
  • Salt and Pepper
  • 2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
  • 1/3 cup brandy or cognac
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • Small handful of flat-leaf parsley

Recipie:

  • Cut fat off of steak, flattening with rolling pin if needed
  • Season steak using salt and pepper
  • Heat olive oil in a pan
  • Sautee steaks for two minutes, flipping halfway through, then allow them to rest
  • Heat olive oil in another pan
  • Add shallots/onions
  • Wait one minute, then add mushrooms, butter, garlic, Worcestershire sauce, and mustard
  • Turn up heat and pour in alcohol- fire will light
  • Once alcohol is gone, move the juice around the pan, then add heavy cream
  • Wait until sauce is thick and then turn off the sauce
  • Reintroduce steaks to the other pan and allow them to cook
  • Serve steak on plate, spooning sauce on top
